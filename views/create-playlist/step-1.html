Instructions.

Choose a mood for you dinner with the slider.

Selecting a romantic mood will generate a calmer playlist with genres such as jazz and balads. Perfect for date night dinners with your partner.

Selecting the party mood will generate a playlist with higher tempo songs, better suited for friday night dinners with your friends.

Leaving the slider button in the middle will give you a playlist somewhere inbetween. Maybe some folk music, classic hits, or something from the top 50.
